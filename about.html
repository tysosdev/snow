About JesterX

JesterX is a unblocked games website with over 140 games, with a simple tab cloak. JesterX was started as a small project , shared between friends. We transferred to a custom made website. After a few rebrandings and redesigns, we've ended up at the JesterX you know and love (unless you work at a school).
